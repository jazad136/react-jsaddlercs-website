Jonathan A. Saddler, Ph. D.

East Carolina University

CSCI 4230/4231 - Spring 2021
Course Syllabus

I led Section 001 of the Computer Science Department's Spring 2021, 16-week Software Engineering Capstone course. This 15-week course was designed to lead senior-level undergraduates through a teamwork-intensive semester of understanding how to build enterprise grade software. Twenty-eight students total were broken into 6 teams of 5-6 members after an initial "get to know your idea" presentation session, and through weekly team-oriented brainstorming sessions, were tasked with documenting and developing a product of their own choosing, as well as documenting its high-level goals and its architecture. As part of our course learning goals, students were to adopt well-known agile software development processes to maintain a steady workflow, and employ state-of-the-art tools (such as Atlassian Jira, Atlassian Confluence, and GitLab) to complete tasks posted as assignments periodically throughout the semester.
